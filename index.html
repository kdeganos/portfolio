About

Kevin Deganos is currently a student at Epicodus, a vocational school for computer programming in Portland, Oregon.

Kevin has previously worked as a social worker for Kern County in California. Before that he worked as a lawyer and is currently licensed to practice law in California.

Kevin enjoys travelling to experience new cultures and environments. He has lived for about one year in the Philippines as well as three months in Japan. Most recently, in 2016, he spent five weeks travelling in Peru and Colombia.
